Lacquer cutting, DMM mastering, electroforming & stamper services
⚡ Why This Matters for NORP The faster a lacquer goes from cutting to electroplating, the higher the fidelity. Lacquer grooves begin healing immediately after cutting. Plants with in-house plating (like Welcome to 1979) can plate within minutes. When using external services, ship lacquers overnight and communicate urgency. NORP should prioritize partners who understand this time sensitivity.

Plating Process Reference
1-Step Plating
Lacquer → Stamper (direct). No backup. If stamper is damaged during pressing, new lacquer cut required. Cheapest option.
2-Step Plating
Lacquer → Father → Mother → Stamper. Mother preserved — additional stampers can be made. Standard for most runs.
3-Step Plating
Lacquer → Father → Mother → Stamper. Father archived for generations. Dozens of mothers possible. Best for catalog titles that repress.
Key material: MDC lacquers (Japanese manufacturer) considered best. Inco S-Round nickel pellets = highest grade for electroforming. Delamo Chemicals silver formula = industry standard since 1980s. RO (reverse osmosis) water preferred over DI for purity.
