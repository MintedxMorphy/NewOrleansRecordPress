Recommended call order for urgent black PVC sourcing
1. Westlake — Push existing rep. Check all 4 MS plants + Matamoros for stock.
2. Precision / GZ Media — World's largest presser, massive PVC purchasing power.
3. United Record Pressing — Largest US plant, bulk buyer.
4. Microforum — Same WarmTone press, compatible compound.
5. Quality Record Pressings / Acoustic Sounds — Multiple presses, own compound tech.
6. Vantiva/Conectiv (Guadalajara) — 30 presses, likely large PVC reserves.
7. Record Compounds (NL) or Dugdale/Benvic (UK/FR) — Specialists, can air freight.
8. Thai Vinyl Compounds — Major global supplier, ~50% of US market.
9. VRMA network — NORP appeared in their 2025 video. Plant operators share supply chain intel.
