Supply Chain Alerts
🔴 China PVC Export Rebate Canceled — April 1, 2026 China eliminated its 13% PVC export rebate. Chinese PVC to US is now more expensive. Plants relying on imported Chinese compound will see cost increases.
🔴 INEOS Closing 2 German PVC Plants Citing energy costs and lack of tariff protection. Reduces European supply — may push EU buyers toward US/Asian suppliers, tightening global availability.
🟡 Strait of Hormuz Disruption US-Israel-Iran conflict disrupting shipping. ICIS reports Middle East petrochemical feedstocks tightening. Indirect impact on PVC precursors.
🟡 Westlake Fuel Surcharges — April 1, 2026 Westlake Chemicals (NORP's primary PVC supplier) implementing fuel surcharges on all shipments. Adds to delivered cost.
🟢 US Domestic Supply Remains Adequate Despite global disruptions, US domestic PVC production is stable. Westlake has 4 Mississippi plants + Matamoros. Housing weakness keeping industrial demand low = more supply available for specialty uses like vinyl records.
Tariff Impact on NORP
⚠ The Core Problem Finished vinyl records are EXEMPT from US tariffs (protected as "informational materials").
Raw materials to MAKE records (PVC, nickel, paperboard, steel) are SUBJECT to tariffs.

This means a record pressed in Germany or Czech Republic has lower input costs than one pressed in the US. GZ Media (Czech) and Optimal Media (Germany) benefit directly from this asymmetry.
Finished vinyl records (import) 0% tariff exempt
PVC compound (import) Elevated tariff↑ cost to NORP
Nickel for electroplating Elevated tariff↑ cost to NORP
Paperboard (jackets/sleeves) Tariffed↑ packaging cost
VRMA is actively lobbying Washington to fix this asymmetry. NORP should participate in advocacy. Contact: VRMA board via Making Vinyl conference.
Competitive Landscape
🟡 Capacity Surplus = Price War Risk US pressing capacity now exceeds demand. Plants dropping to 1–2 shifts. "Dead air" discounts appearing. Compete on speed, quality, and service — not just price.
🟢 Softwax (Philadelphia) CLOSED — Oct 2024 Bankruptcy + equipment failure. Their clients need a new plant. Opportunity for NORP to pick up displaced indie labels.
🟢 Classic City Vinyl Works (Athens, GA) — CLOSED Left a gap in the Southeast. Atlanta labels and Nashville independents looking for alternatives. NORP is well-positioned geographically.
🟡 GZ Media Expanding US Presence World's largest presser (Czech Republic) is acquiring US operations. Using inter-company structures to route materials and bypass tariffs. Watch for further acquisitions.
